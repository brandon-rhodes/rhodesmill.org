Essays, yes. Short stories, poems, yes. But never another full-length book.

He returned to his endless work with Elven legend, history, and language. This was no longer a secret — and he showed the drafts to his publisher —

But he was writing for himself.

The Silmarillion never went through the wrenching process of being critiqued and reworked to face an audience.
