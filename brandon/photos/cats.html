Persephone (Persie)

6 Weeks

Περσεφόνε — Greek goddess of Spring

My sister rescued Persie from an apartment laundry room where the thin kitten had been mewing for help. The kitten could not drink — she could only lap wet cat food off a finger — so the veterinarian guessed that she was five weeks old. After being given her shots, Persie was given to me, and a friend suggested that the springtime kitten be named Persephone.

It was only her second or third day home when I woke up to find Persie surveying the den from atop her scratching post. In case anyone thinks I cruelly stranded her atop the post for the sake of taking the picture, here is a movie of Persie climbing her post.
