Brandon Craig Rhodes
Essential Library

or, How to Look at the World Like Brandon Does

It is my belief that a literate mind is largely the product of the literature it has consumed. This at least seems true of me - and whatever is true of ourselves we easily assume to be true of others! For many the intellectual primacy of the written may already have fallen before the many alternative media of the modern world. And doubtless components of my own thought derive from the cinema and the television screen;
