Massachusetts

[1 July 1999] On Thursday the Fox reported in briefly from the state of Massachusetts, only three states from the end of the Trail (with Vermont, New Hampshire, and Maine remaining). He was visiting Great Barrington, which he had reached from the Trail's intersection with US 7.

Church in New York?

[26 June 1999] Today (Saturday) Bobby reported in from Pawling, New York. He states that he has crossed Conneticut Route 55, and the trail had temporarily taken him back across the border into New York. He was planning on taking a train into New York City to visit and attend church there Sunday morning.
