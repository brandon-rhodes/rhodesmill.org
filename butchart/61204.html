Harvey Butchart map: Granite Park Quadrangle, Arizona, 7.5 minute topographical map, 1967.

As you zoom and scroll the map below, its data will load directly from the servers of the Cline Library at Northern Arizona University which hosts the Harvey Butchart Collection — the maps and trail logs of famed Grand Canyon backpacker Harvey Butchart (1907–2002) who taught in their mathematics department.

I offer this page because I find the modern OpenSeadragon image viewer, used below, makes zooming and scrolling easier than on the Cline Library’s web site. Here is their official page for this map: http://archive.library.nau.edu/cdm/ref/collection/cpa/id/61204
